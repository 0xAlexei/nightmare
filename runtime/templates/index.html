Welcome to the Nightmare Fuzzing Project application! It was created by Joxean Koret as a proof of concept distributed fuzzing framework (with central administration) for LaCon 2013 (an underground European conference). It was used, later on, to fuzz many products like AV engines, office suites, document viewers, etc...
